Chapter 1

The Perfect Life

Alicia Berenson lived in one of the most desirable neighborhoods in London. Her house, a grand Victorian with large windows that overlooked the park, was the envy of many. She was a successful painter, her works displayed in galleries across the city. Her husband, Gabriel, was an in-demand fashion photographer, his career skyrocketing in recent years.

To the outside world, they were the perfect couple. They attended gallery openings together, hosted dinner parties for London's elite, and traveled to exotic locations for Gabriel's photoshoots. But behind closed doors, their life was far from perfect.

Alicia had been struggling with her mental health for years. The pressure to create new works, to maintain their public image, was taking its toll. Gabriel, consumed by his career, was often absent, both physically and emotionally.

On the surface, everything appeared normal. The house was always immaculate, the garden perfectly manicured. But Alicia felt like she was living in a gilded cage, trapped by expectations and a marriage that was slowly unraveling.

That fateful evening, when Gabriel returned home late from another photoshoot, something inside Alicia snapped. The argument started as it always did - with accusations and defensiveness. But this time, it ended with five gunshots that would change everything.
